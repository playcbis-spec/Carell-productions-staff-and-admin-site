Cannon

Founder, Director & Architect

From back where I can remember, my mom was always singing around me and doing artsy, musical things in our home. Growing up in that creative atmosphere, I naturally picked it up right away and started diving into music from a very young age.

As I grew, my passion expanded into acting and film directing, allowing me to start bringing visual stories to life. From there, I discovered a love for structural design and got heavily into architecture.

Today, I am a multi-talented arts and architecture 14-year-old upcoming professional, leading development on original productions and designs.
